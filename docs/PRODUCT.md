# StudentOS Product Definition

StudentOS is a school planning system that connects subjects, chapters,
homework, exams, study materials, and daily revision.

## Core promise

> Open StudentOS and know what to study next, why it matters,
> and which material to use.

## StudentOS is not primarily

- a Pomodoro timer
- a social study tracker
- a leaderboard
- a generic AI chatbot
- a file-storage app

## Core workflow

Subjects and chapters
→ homework and exams
→ connected study resources
→ daily revision plan
→ focused work
→ confidence and weak-topic tracking
